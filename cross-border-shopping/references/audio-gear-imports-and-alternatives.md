# Audio Gear Imports & US Alternatives

## Huawei FreeClip 2 — Import Status

- **Released:** September 2025 (global), not sold officially in US (Huawei trade ban)
- **Global MSRP:** ~£179 (~$230)
- **Import prices seen:** Giztop ~$249, Microless comparable
- **Key specs:** C-bridge clip-on open-ear, 10.8mm dual-diaphragm, BT 6.0 (unratified spec — listing may be aspirational), IP57, 9h buds / 38h with case
- **Software dependency:** Requires Huawei AI Life app (sideloaded in US) for firmware updates and EQ
